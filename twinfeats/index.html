About Us

twin brothers Kent and Kevin Smotherman founded Twinfeats Software in 1983 with a simple premise: Free Software for a free world. From the beginnings on the Coomodore 64b to today's Android apps, that vision has never wavered

Kent Smotherman has 38 years of professional experience using over a dozen programming languages. He enjoys teaching web development, chess, and petting the family cat, Solo, though he is not a Bond villian

Kevin Smotherman has 30 years of professional deveopment experience. His current role is a lead/advisory software development engineer at CSG International. His favorite day of the year is ground hog's day. No one knows
